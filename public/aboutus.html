Boat Jetty Rd, Thathampally, Alappuzha, Kerala 688013, India

Alleppey , also know as Alappuzha is an important tourist destination in kerala, India that is situated 62 kilometres (39 mi) to the south of Kochi and 155 kilometres (96 mi) north of Trivandrum.

The Backwaters of Alappuzha are the most popular tourist attraction in Kerala & also includes picturesque canals, backwaters, beaches, and lagoons. A houseboat cruise in these backwaters helps you take a ride that connects Kumarakom and Cochin to the North and Quilon to the South.Alappuzha is the access point for the annual Nehru Trophy Boat Race, held on the Punnamada Lake, near Alappuzha, on the second Saturday of August every year. This is the most competitive and popular of the boat races in India.
